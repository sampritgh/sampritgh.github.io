About me

I am a PhD candidate in the Department of Mathematics, University of Toronto since 2017. I am a part of the research team at GANITA Lab and my supervisor is : Prof. V. Kumar Murty

My primary research is in Analytic Number Theory and Arithmetic Geometry.

I am also interested in applications of number theory in Cryptography, Cybersecurity and related areas.
In 2019-20, I was involved with the Smart Villges project focusing on privacy and security of medical data in a distributed e-health architecture. I am currently attending The Fields Institute's Cyber Connexion Program.

Before coming to Toronto, I attended IIT Bombay and my advisor was Prof. Sudhir Ghorpade.
