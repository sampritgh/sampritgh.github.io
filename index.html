About me

I am a Postdoctoral Fellow at the Department of Mathematics and Statistics, University of Calgary, working with Khoa Nguyen and Thomas Bitoun.

I earned my PhD in November 2023, at the University of Toronto. I was a part of the research team at GANITA Lab and my supervisor was Prof. V. Kumar Murty.

My area of research is Number Theory, primarily analytic number theory. I wrote my thesis on Higher Euler-Kronecker constants. I am also interested in Diophantine Approximation, Arithmetic Geometry and applications of number theory in Cryptography, Cybersecurity and related areas.

Before coming to Toronto, I attended IIT Bombay and my advisor was Prof. Sudhir Ghorpade.
