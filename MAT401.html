MAT401Y : Polynomial Equations and Fields
Summer 2023

Course Description

The central goal of this course is to show that there is no general formula to determine the roots of a quintic polynomial, i.e. degree is 5.
But we need to build up some theory to get there. The topics we will be learning are :

  • Commutative rings, Domains, ring maps, ideals, quotient rings, isomorphism theorems.
  • Polynomial rings, roots of polynomials, irreducibility.
  • Fields, Normal and separable extensions, Galois theory.
  • Radical and solvable extensions, insolvability of quintics by radicals.
  • Constructibility.

Textbook : Galois Theory by Joseph Rotman, Second edition, Springer. ISBN 0-387-98541-7
